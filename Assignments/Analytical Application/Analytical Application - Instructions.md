# Analytical Application (15%)

**Length:** 500–600 words *or equivalent*  
**Sources:** 1–2 academic sources (APA format)  
**Due:** Week 5  

---

## Purpose

This assignment helps you:
- Understand key ideas in **positive psychology**  
- Apply them to **real academic or career situations**  
- Communicate your ideas using **academic language and evidence**  

---

## Task (Flexible Options)

You will **explain one theory** and **apply it to a real situation**.

You can choose **how you present your work**:

### Option A – Written Paragraph (Traditional)
- 500–600 words  

### Option B – Audio or Video Explanation
- 3–5 minutes  
- Include a short reference list (written)  

### Option C – Visual + Written (e.g., Slides or Infographic)
- 6–8 slides *or* 1-page visual  
- Include short written explanations (approx. 300–400 words total)  

All options must include:
- Clear explanation of a theory  
- Application to a real situation  
- 1–2 academic sources (APA)

---

## Step 1: Choose a Theory

Select **one**:

- PERMA  
- Broaden-and-Build Theory  
- Self-Determination Theory  
- Stress, resilience, or self-compassion  
- Character strengths  

---

## Step 2: Explain the Theory

- What is the theory?  
- What are its key concepts?  
- Use **course vocabulary**  

---

## Step 3: Apply the Theory

Choose a **real situation**, such as:

- Stress during exams  
- Motivation in school  
- Group work challenges  
- Work–life balance  
- Career planning  

Explain:
- How the theory helps explain the situation  
- How it could improve well-being or success  

---

## Step 4: Use Evidence

- Include **1–2 academic sources** (course readings are recommended)  
- Use **APA citation**  

---

## Learning Outcomes (CLOs)

This assignment helps you:

- Explain and apply **positive psychology theories**  
- Use **academic vocabulary** accurately  
- Integrate **academic sources**  
- Apply well-being concepts to **real-life contexts**  

---

## Assessment Criteria

You will be assessed on:

- Understanding of the theory  
- Application to a real situation  
- Use of evidence  
- Use of academic vocabulary  
- Clarity and organization  

---

## Support Options (UDL)

To help you succeed, you can:

- Review **lecture slides, readings, or videos**  
- Use **models or examples** provided in class  
- Discuss your ideas with a partner before writing/recording  
- Submit a **draft or outline** for feedback (optional)  
- Use AI tools **ethically** to brainstorm or check clarity (Week 10)  

---

## Tips for Success

- Be **specific** (avoid very general examples)  
- Use key terms like *flourishing, autonomy, resilience*  
- Focus on **explaining why and how**, not just describing  
- Keep your work clear and organized
