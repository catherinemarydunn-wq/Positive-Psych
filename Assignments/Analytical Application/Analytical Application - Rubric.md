# Analytical Application Rubric (15%)

## Overview
This rubric applies to all formats (written, audio/video, visual).  
Focus is on **ideas, clarity, and application**, not the format you choose.

---

## Criteria and Levels

### 1. Understanding of Theory (4 marks)

| Level | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **4 – Excellent** | Clear, accurate explanation of the theory; key concepts are fully explained and correctly used |
| **3 – Good** | Mostly clear explanation; minor errors or missing detail |
| **2 – Developing** | Basic understanding; some confusion or inaccurate use of concepts |
| **1 – Beginning** | Limited or incorrect understanding of the theory |

---

### 2. Application to Real Context (4 marks)

| Level | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **4 – Excellent** | Strong, specific example; clearly explains how the theory applies and improves the situation |
| **3 – Good** | Clear example; some explanation of application but could be deeper |
| **2 – Developing** | Example is general or unclear; limited connection to theory |
| **1 – Beginning** | Little or no clear application to a real situation |

---

### 3. Use of Evidence (3 marks)

| Level | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **3 – Excellent** | 1–2 appropriate academic sources used effectively; correct APA citation |
| **2 – Developing** | Sources included but may be unclear, weakly integrated, or have APA errors |
| **1 – Beginning** | Missing sources or incorrect use of evidence |

---

### 4. Use of Academic Vocabulary (2 marks)

| Level | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **2 – Excellent** | Accurate and appropriate use of key course vocabulary (e.g., flourishing, autonomy, resilience) |
| **1 – Developing** | Some use of key terms, but with errors or limited range |
| **0 – Beginning** | Minimal or incorrect use of academic vocabulary |

---

### 5. Clarity and Organization (2 marks)

| Level | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **2 – Excellent** | Ideas are clear, well-organized, and easy to follow (in any format) |
| **1 – Developing** | Some organization, but ideas may be unclear or uneven |
| **0 – Beginning** | Difficult to follow; lacks clear structure |

---

## Total: /15

---

## Notes for Different Formats (UDL)

- **Written:** Clear paragraphs, logical flow, correct citations  
- **Audio/Video:** Clear speaking, organized ideas, references included  
- **Visual:** Clear structure, readable text, ideas explained (not just listed)  

---

## What Success Looks Like

A strong assignment:
- Clearly explains a theory using course terms  
- Applies it to a **specific, realistic situation**  
- Uses **at least one course reading**  
- Is **clear, focused, and easy to follow**
