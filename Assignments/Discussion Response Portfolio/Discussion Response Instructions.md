# Discussion Responses (20%)

## Overview

Students will complete **four discussion responses** throughout the course.

These responses are designed to:

- support understanding of course concepts  
- develop critical thinking  
- build academic writing skills  
- connect ideas to real-life experiences  

---

## Requirements

Students must:

- respond to the discussion prompt  
- use ideas from readings and/or class  
- include clear examples  
- demonstrate understanding of key concepts  

---

## Length

- **150–250 words per response**

---

## Submission

- Submitted through [LMS / Blackboard]  
- Students may revise early responses based on feedback (optional, instructor choice)

---

## Grading Criteria

| Criteria | Description |
|--------|------------|
| Understanding | Demonstrates understanding of key concepts |
| Application | Applies ideas to examples or real situations |
| Critical Thinking | Shows reflection, analysis, or evaluation |
| Clarity | Writing is clear and organized |

---

## Important Notes for Students

- Focus on **ideas, not perfection**  
- Use your own words  
- Support your opinion with reasons  
- Keep your writing clear and simple  

---

## Weight

- 4 responses = 20% total (5% each)
