# GPSE 100: Assessment Overview, Instructions, and Rubrics (Revised)

---

## Assessment Breakdown

- **Analytical Application – 15%**  
- **Research Critique – 15%**  
- **Mini Inquiry Project – 25%**  
- **Intervention Project – 25%**  
- **Academic Discussion Portfolio – 20%**  

---

## 1. Analytical Application (15%)

**Length:** 500–600 words  
**Sources:** 1–2 academic sources (APA)  

**Task:**  
Explain a theoretical framework and apply it to a real academic or career context.  

Students should use key course terminology accurately.

---

## 2. Research Critique (15%)

**Length:** 400–500 words  
**Source:** One provided study  

**Task:**  
Evaluate:
- Research question  
- Method  
- Correlation vs. causation  
- Limitations  
- Bias and/or ethical considerations  

---

## 3. Mini Inquiry Project (25%)

**Groups:** 2–3 students  

- **Report:** 700–900 words (15%)  
- **Presentation:** 5 minutes (10%)  

**Task:**  
Design and conduct a small-scale inquiry with ethical reflection.

---

## 4. Intervention Project (25%)

**Groups:** 2–3 students  

- **Report:** 800–1000 words (15%)  
- **Presentation:** 5–6 minutes (10%)  

**Task:**  
Design an evidence-informed intervention that includes:
- Theoretical grounding  
- Cultural considerations  

---

## 5. Academic Discussion Portfolio (20%)

- 4 responses  
- 200–250 words each  

**Task:**  
Engage with course readings by:
- Responding thoughtfully to prompts  
- Interacting with peers  
- Using academic vocabulary accurately and appropriately
