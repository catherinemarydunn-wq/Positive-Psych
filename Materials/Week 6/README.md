
# Week 6 – Strengths and Grit

## Overview

This week explores character strengths and their role in well-being. Students are introduced to the idea that individuals have core strengths that can support personal growth and success.

The week also examines the concept of grit, a popular idea in psychology that emphasizes perseverance. Students will consider both the value and the limitations of grit, including how social and structural factors influence success.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Define character strengths  
- Explain how strengths are connected to well-being  
- Describe the concept of grit  
- Evaluate the strengths and limitations of grit

## Note to Students

This week introduces both a key concept (character strengths) and a critical perspective (grit). While strengths are widely used in positive psychology, the idea of grit has been debated.

As you read, think about how personal qualities like perseverance interact with external factors such as opportunity, support, and context.
