# Week 12 – Inquiry Presentations

## Overview

This week is dedicated to presenting your inquiry projects. Students will share their research questions, study design, results, and interpretations.

The focus is on clear communication, thoughtful interpretation, and engagement with the audience.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Present their research clearly and effectively  
- Explain their design and results  
- Interpret findings thoughtfully  
- Respond to questions about their work  

---

## Readings

- No new readings  

---

## Note to Students

This week is an opportunity to demonstrate your understanding of the inquiry process. Your goal is to communicate your ideas clearly, not to present perfect results.

Focus on:

- explaining your thinking  
- describing your results accurately  
- acknowledging limitations  

It is normal to feel nervous. Preparation and clarity are more important than perfection.

---

## Assessment

- **Inquiry Presentation**
- **Inquiry Report due**
