# Week 4 – Positive Emotions and Gratitude

## Overview

This week focuses on the role of positive emotions in well-being, with particular attention to gratitude. Students explore how positive emotions influence thinking, learning, and resilience, and examine research on gratitude as a psychological intervention.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Explain the broaden-and-build theory of positive emotions  
- Describe how gratitude is connected to well-being  
- Understand how psychological interventions are studied  
- Reflect on the role of gratitude in their own lives
