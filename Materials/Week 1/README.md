# Week 1 – Introduction to Positive Psychology

## Overview

This week introduces the field of positive psychology and explores the shift from a focus on mental illness to a focus on well-being and human flourishing. Students are introduced to key concepts, foundational vocabulary, and critical perspectives on the field.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Define positive psychology  
- Distinguish between happiness and flourishing  
- Identify key goals of positive psychology  
- Recognize potential critiques of the field
