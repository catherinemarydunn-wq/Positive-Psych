# Week 10 – Inquiry Workshop

## Overview

This week focuses on developing your inquiry project. Students will work on designing their study, refining their research questions, and beginning analysis.

Class time will be used for guided work, feedback, and support.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Refine their research question  
- Develop their study design  
- Begin analyzing their data  
- Interpret initial results  

---

## Readings

- No required readings  

---

## Note to Students

This week is focused on your inquiry project. You will apply the concepts from previous weeks to your own study.

Be prepared to ask questions, share ideas, and revise your work. You do not need to have everything finished—this is a working week.

---

## Assessment

- Work on Inquiry Project
