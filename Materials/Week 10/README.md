# Week 10 – Interpreting Results and Preparing Presentations

## Overview

This week focuses on interpreting the results of your inquiry project and preparing to present your findings. Students will work on analyzing their results, identifying key patterns, and developing clear explanations.

Class time will be used for guided work, feedback, and preparation for presentations.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Interpret the results of their study  
- Describe patterns and relationships in their data  
- Explain findings clearly and accurately  
- Begin preparing their presentation

## Note to Students

This week is focused on making sense of your results and preparing to communicate them clearly. You are not expected to have perfect results. Instead, focus on explaining what your data shows and what it might mean.

Be careful not to overstate your findings. Remember that your results are based on a simulation and reflect your assumptions.
