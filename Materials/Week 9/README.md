# Week 9 – Methods, Simulation, and AI

## Overview

This week focuses on how to design and carry out your inquiry project. Students are introduced to key ideas in research design, including how to create a research question, define variables, and plan a simple study.

The week also introduces simulation as a way of generating data and explores how AI can be used responsibly to support the research process.

This is a transition from learning about psychology to applying it through your own project.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Develop a clear and focused research question  
- Identify and define key variables  
- Understand how a simple study is designed  
- Explain what a simulation is and how it is used in research  
- Use AI tools appropriately to support academic work
