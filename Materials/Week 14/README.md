# Week 14 – Intervention Presentations and Course Reflection

## Overview

This week concludes the course with presentations of your intervention projects. Students will share their ideas, explain their design, and reflect on how course concepts informed their work.

The week also provides an opportunity to reflect on learning across the course and consider how these ideas can be applied beyond the classroom.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Present their intervention clearly and effectively  
- Explain how course concepts informed their design  
- Reflect on their learning and development  
- Evaluate the strengths and limitations of their intervention  

---

## Readings

- No new readings  

---

## Note to Students

This week is an opportunity to share your work and reflect on your learning. Focus on explaining your ideas clearly and connecting them to what you have learned in the course.

Think about how your understanding of well-being, motivation, and research has developed over time.
