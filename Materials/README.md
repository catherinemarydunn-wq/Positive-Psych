# GPSE 100 – Reading Materials

## Overview

This folder contains all readings used in the course.

The readings are:

- adapted for CEFR B2-level students  
- selected to support weekly topics and assessments  
- designed to build both content knowledge and academic skills  

---

## How to Use the Readings

Each week includes 1–2 short readings.

Instructors should:

1. Assign readings before class (when possible)  
2. Use readings as a basis for:
   - discussion  
   - small-group activities  
   - critical thinking tasks  

3. Encourage students to:
   - identify key ideas  
   - ask questions  
   - connect readings to their own experiences  

---

## Types of Readings

This course includes a mix of:

### 1. Adapted Textbook Excerpts
- Based on *Hart (2021): Positive Psychology: The Basics*  
- Simplified for accessibility  

### 2. Adapted Research Articles
- Introduce students to empirical research  
- Used to develop research literacy  

### 3. Concept Guides
- Explain key ideas (e.g., PERMA, SDT, flow)  
- Written in clear, student-friendly language  

### 4. Critical Perspectives
- Encourage students to question and evaluate ideas  
- Introduce cultural and structural considerations
