
# Week 8 – Goals and Flow

## Overview

This week focuses on goal setting and flow, two concepts that are closely connected to motivation, performance, and well-being. Students explore how setting clear and meaningful goals can support focus and persistence, and how the experience of flow can enhance engagement and satisfaction.

This week marks a transition from theory to application, as students begin to apply psychological concepts to their own learning and project work.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Explain how goal setting influences motivation and performance  
- Identify characteristics of effective goals  
- Describe the concept of flow and optimal experience  
- Connect goal setting and flow to well-being and academic success
