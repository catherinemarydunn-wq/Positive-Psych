
# Week 7 – Motivation and Self-Determination Theory

## Overview

This week explores motivation and its role in learning and well-being. Students are introduced to Self-Determination Theory (SDT), a major theory in psychology that explains how different types of motivation influence behavior.

The week focuses on the difference between intrinsic and extrinsic motivation and examines how the basic psychological needs for autonomy, competence, and relatedness support engagement and well-being.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Define motivation and explain why it matters  
- Distinguish between intrinsic and extrinsic motivation  
- Explain the three basic psychological needs in SDT  
- Describe how motivation is connected to well-being
