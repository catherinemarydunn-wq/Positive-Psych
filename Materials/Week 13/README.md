# Week 13 – Intervention Project Workshop

## Overview

This week is dedicated to developing your intervention project. Students will apply concepts from the course to design a positive psychology intervention that supports well-being.

Class time will be used for guided work, feedback, and refinement of ideas.

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this week, students should be able to:

- Develop a clear and focused intervention idea  
- Apply course concepts to support their design  
- Explain how and why their intervention should be effective  
- Identify potential limitations  

---

## Readings

- No new readings  

---

## Note to Students

This week is focused on applying what you have learned throughout the course. Use this time to develop your intervention, connect it to course concepts, and refine your ideas.

Focus on clarity and purpose. A strong intervention is not necessarily complex—it is thoughtful, realistic, and clearly explained.
