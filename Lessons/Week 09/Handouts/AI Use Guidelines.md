## Using AI in Academic Work

### AI Can Help You:

- generate ideas  
- organize information  
- create drafts  

---

### But You Must:

- check all information  
- use your own thinking  
- revise what AI produces  

---

### You SHOULD NOT:

- copy AI answers without editing  
- submit AI work as your own  
- trust AI without checking  

---

### Key Idea

AI is a tool—not a replacement for your thinking.

---

### Reflection

How will you use AI responsibly?
