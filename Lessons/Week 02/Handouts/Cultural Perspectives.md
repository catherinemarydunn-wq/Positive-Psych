## Cultural Perspectives on Well-Being

### Indigenous Teachings (Medicine Wheel)

Well-being = balance between:

- Mental  
- Physical  
- Emotional  
- Spiritual  

---

### Seven Grandfather Teachings (examples)

- Respect  
- Honesty  
- Courage  
- Love  

---

### Key Idea

Well-being is not only about feeling good.  
It is about balance, relationships, and community.

---

### Joshanloo (2014)

Western view:
- happiness = feeling good  

Other perspectives:
- balance  
- harmony  
- acceptance
