## What Matters Most in a Good Life?

### Step 1: Rank (Individual)

Rank these from MOST important (1) to LEAST important (6):

___ Happiness  
___ Success  
___ Relationships  
___ Meaning  
___ Health  
___ Money  

---

### Step 2: Compare (Pairs)

Discuss with a partner:

- What is the same?
- What is different?
- Why did you choose your top 3?

---

### Step 3: Share (Class)

Be ready to explain ONE choice.
