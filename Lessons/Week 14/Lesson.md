# Week 14 Lesson Plan  
## Intervention Presentations and Course Reflection  
**Duration:** 3 hours (with 15-minute break)  
**Model:** Adapted 4 A’s (Activate → Prepare → Present → Reflect)

---

## Learning Objectives (Student-Friendly)

By the end of this class, students will be able to:

- present their intervention clearly and confidently  
- explain how their intervention is connected to course concepts  
- reflect on their learning across the course  
- identify key ideas they can apply in real life  

---

## 🟢 ACTIVATE (15 minutes)

### 1. Opening + Tone Setting (10 min)

Set expectations:

- Presentations are about **communication, not perfection**  
- Focus on:
  - clarity  
  - realism  
  - connection to course ideas  

---

### 2. Quick Warm-Up (5 min)

Prompt:

> What is one idea from this course that stayed with you?

Students share briefly in pairs.

---

## 🔵 PREPARE (15 minutes)

### 3. Final Preparation Time (15 min)

Students:

- review slides  
- confirm speaking roles  
- clarify key points  

---

### Instructor role:

- check:
  - clarity of intervention  
  - connection to theory  
  - timing  

---

## ☕ BREAK (15 minutes)

---

## 🟡 PRESENT (90 minutes)

### 4. Intervention Presentations

Each group:

- 5–7 minutes presentation  
- 2–3 minutes questions  

---

### Suggested Structure for Students

1. Problem  
2. Intervention  
3. Why it works (course concepts)  
4. Expected results  

---

### Audience Role

Students complete **peer feedback sheets**

---

### Instructor Role

- keep time  
- ask 1–2 guiding questions if needed  
- support quieter groups  

---

## 🔴 REFLECT (45 minutes)

### 5. Whole-Class Reflection (20 min)

Discuss:

- What types of interventions were most effective?  
- What surprised you?  
- What was challenging?  

---

### 6. Individual Reflection (15 min)

Students write:

> What is one thing you learned in this course?  
> What is one idea you will use in real life?

---

### 7. Course Wrap-Up (10 min)

Instructor highlights:

- key themes:
  - well-being is complex  
  - context matters  
  - small actions can have impact  

---

## 🧠 After Class

- All Intervention Projects submitted  
- Course complete  

---

## 🎯 Materials Needed

- Peer feedback sheets  
- Timer  
- Optional reflection sheet  

---

## 💡 Instructor Notes

- Keep tone:
  👉 supportive and calm  
- Avoid over-interrupting presentations  
- Encourage:
  👉 respectful listening  
  👉 thoughtful questions  

---

## 🔥 Core Goals of This Week

1. Demonstrate learning  
2. Build confidence  
3. Reflect on growth  

---

## ⚡ Energy Strategy

- vary presenters  
- keep timing tight  
- maintain audience engagement  

---

## 🎯 Role of This Week

This week:

- consolidates learning  
- celebrates student work  
- creates closure for the course
