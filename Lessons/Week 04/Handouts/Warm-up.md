## Think About a Positive Moment

### Step 1: Individual Reflection

Think about a time when you felt:

- happy  
- calm  
- excited  
- proud  

Write about it:

What happened?

________________________________________

How did you feel?

________________________________________

---

### Step 2: Pair Discussion

Discuss with a partner:

- What did you do during this time?
- Did you think differently?
