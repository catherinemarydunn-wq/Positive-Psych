# GPSE 100 – Positive Psychology for Academic and Career Success

## Overview

This course introduces students to key concepts in positive psychology and helps them apply these ideas to academic success and everyday life.

Students explore topics such as:

- well-being and flourishing  
- motivation and goal setting  
- stress and self-compassion  
- research and critical thinking  
- real-world interventions  

---

## How to Use This Course

Each week includes:

- a full lesson plan  
- student handouts  
- suggested activities  

### To teach a class:

1. Open the folder for that week  
2. Review the lesson plan  
3. Print or share the handouts  
4. Follow the lesson structure  

---

## Course Structure

- 14 weeks  
- 3 hours per week  
- CEFR B2 level  

---

## Assessments

- Analytical Application (15%)  
- Research Critique (15%)  
- Inquiry Project (25%)  
- Intervention Project (25%)  
- Discussion Responses (20%)  

---

## Notes for Instructors

- This course is discussion-based and interactive  
- Focus on application, not memorization  
- Keep explanations simple and concrete  
- Encourage student participation
